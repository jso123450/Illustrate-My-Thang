Illustrate My Thang is a remake of OMGPOP's hit game, Draw My Thing!. It is currently being developed by students as their final project for Software Development. The team is composed of: Ho Yin Ho, Johnny So, Javis Wu, and Kah Soon Yap.
- Server Message
The game works as follows:
  1. Upon three people connecting, the game will start.
  2. The first player to connect will be assigned as the drawer for the round.
  3. Only the round's drawer will be able to draw on the canvas and utilize the drawing tools.
  4. The round's drawer is assigned a word to draw it on the canvas by using the given, various tools.
  5. Whoever can guess the word in the time limit will be given points. However, the first person to guess will be given extra.
  6. The drawer is awarded points as well, if anybody is able to guess the word (only once per round).
  7. When the countdown hits 0, a new round will begin.
  8. When two rounds have passed, the game is over, and the person who has the most points at the end will win.
